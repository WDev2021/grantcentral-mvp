GrantCentral.us

GrantCentral will make government grants, subsidies, incentives, and funding opportunities public and easily accessible to everyone — especially entrepreneurs, startups, small businesses, and innovators.

We want the hundreds of billions of dollars in public funds to be used to boost our communities' wellbeing through real entrepreneurship — rather than going into fraud, waste, and abuse.
